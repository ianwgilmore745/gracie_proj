Hi Baby,

September might have been my favorite month in the last year. I got to be there for your homecoming game and see you on court. I got to go to the aquarium with you. I got to get ready with you for you homecoming dance and take pre-dance photos with you. It was a really special time and I was so happy to be there for it. As amazing as it was though, leaving you that night was one of the most difficult things I have done. I still remember crying in front of the mirror with you knowing that I had to leave in less than half an hour. I remember seeing the video of you and misty and the photo of you in the bathroom without me before my flight. As the plane was taking off I was thinking about you at the dance and I just wanted to be there with you. Long distance is hard, not just because I have to leave you and I can't be physically there with you. But because I have to miss so many big moments and milestones for you. I hate missing them, I wish I could have been there for so many things this year. But I am still so grateful to have you in any way I can. I will get to be there for some huge moments in the next few years and I am so excited for it.

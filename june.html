My love,

This june was the best birthday I could ever have. I got the best birthday cake ever delivered to me by my gorgeous girlfriend in a dress. But the month had a lot more to it. Our early mornings trying to catch the sunrise at lizards mouth. Being eaten alive by flies, going to the beach for shaved ice, going to the cages, our Sally and McQueen keychains. You made the month so memorable and I'm so grateful for all the pictures you had us take. While making this I just kept realizing that I understand taking a lot of pictures now. It doesn't ruin the moment but when we look back at them we get to remember the moments associated with them and all kinds of things we may have otherwise forgotten. Thank you for pushing for them, I am always happy to take a photo with you.

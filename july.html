Love,

July was a really fun month. We didn't do quite as many big activities and trips, but we did so much hanging out and spent so much time in each others company. That is some of my favorite time we spend together. As much as I love going out and experiencing the world with you. I am just as happy to cuddle and watch movies and shows. I love watching you with my cats. You are so gentle and sweet with them and it showcases so many of your amazing qualities. I loved our joking around and messing around on the swings at night or making a little video in a parking lot just for fun. As much as I love our celebrations and our trips, our little interactions and moments when nothing else is going on make me the happiest.
